About Us

Nasrin-Safrin Hospital is a premier healthcare institution dedicated to providing high-quality medical services with compassion and excellence. Established in 2002 with the vision of enhancing community health, our hospital combines state-of-the-art facilities with a team of experienced healthcare professionals.

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including pediatric care, gynecology, general medicine, and specialized surgeries, all designed to meet the diverse needs of our patients. Our commitment to patient-centered care ensures that every individual receives personalized attention and treatment tailored to their specific health requirements.

At Nasrin-Safrin Hospital, we believe in fostering a supportive and healing environment, where our patients feel valued and cared for. With a focus on continuous improvement and innovation, we strive to be a leader in healthcare delivery, making a positive impact on the lives of the individuals and families we serve.

Dr. Haja Mohaideen - Pediatrician and Cardiologist

His extensive expertise has made him a trusted figure in the medical community, known for his compassionate care and dedication to children's health. Dr. Irshath Nasreen - Obstetrician and Gynaecologist (OBGY)

MBBS, MS.OG

Dr. Irshath Nasreen is a highly accomplished gynecologist with over nine years of experience and a flawless 0% failure rate in her practice. A gold medalist from the prestigious Madras Medical College, she specializes in TRIAL VH and intrapartum surgeries. Her expertise, combined with a compassionate approach, has made her a trusted choice for women seeking exceptional gynecological care. Dr. Nasreen is dedicated to ensuring the best outcomes for her patients through meticulous attention and personalized treatment.
